## Imaginary Number , i<br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ i^2 = -1$ ဖြစ်စေသော ကိန်းကို imaginary ဟုခေါ်သည်။ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; i = $\sqrt{-1} $ ဟုလည်းရေးသည်။ <br>
## Example <br>
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; Express the following in terns of i : <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $\sqrt{-4},&emsp; \sqrt{-9},&emsp; \sqrt{-25},&emsp; \sqrt{-7},&emsp; \sqrt{-100}, &emsp;\sqrt{13}$ <br>
## Solution <br>
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-4} = \sqrt{4(-1)} = \sqrt{4}\sqrt{-1} = 2i $ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-9} = \sqrt{9(-1)} = \sqrt{9}\sqrt{-1} = 3i $ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-25} = \sqrt{25(-1)} = \sqrt{25}\sqrt{-1} = 5i $ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-7} = \sqrt{7(-1)} = \sqrt{7}\sqrt{-1} = \sqrt{7}i $ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-100} = \sqrt{100(-1)} = \sqrt{100}\sqrt{-1} = 10i $ <br>

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; $ \sqrt{-13} = \sqrt{13(-1)} = \sqrt{13}\sqrt{-1} = \sqrt{13}i $ <br>
